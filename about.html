Coder. Designer. Night Owl.

I enjoy long hours of coding and complex problem solving with the smallest victories making all of those hours worthwhile. Games that require tact and critical thinking are my first choice, especially when they have challenging boss fights.

The soundtrack of my life is sung by my corgi, Maverick, who performs best in the middle of the night when I am trying to sleep.
